Welcome to RAISINS

RAISINS(R and AI Solutions for INferential Statistics) is an advanced, user-friendly online platform for statistical analysis, seamlessly integrating the power of R, Python, and Artificial Intelligence. Specially designed for agricultural research, RAISINS enables students, researchers, and professionals to perform complex analyses and generate publication-ready results without the need for software installation or coding expertise. Simply choose your analysis module, upload your dataset, and receive instant, insightful outputs. Before RAISINS, researchers spent at least four days completing their data analysis..now, you can do it in just four seconds!

Analysis Modules

Doing a statistical analysis has never been easier. Simply select the appropriate analysis module listed below to launch the application. Our platform operates on secure cloud infrastructure to ensure high-performance computing for your data analysis. To support sustained access and development, a modest subscription is required per module. These plans are thoughtfully structured to remain affordable for students and researchers. Once subscribed, access the app and upload your dataset and click Run Analysis the results are generated instantly, with accuracy and clarity.

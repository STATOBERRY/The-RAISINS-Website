Welcome to RAISINS

RAISINS(R and AI Solutions for INferential Statistics) is an advanced, user-friendly online platform for statistical analysis, seamlessly integrating the power of R, Python, and Artificial Intelligence. Specially designed for agricultural research, RAISINS enables students, researchers, and professionals to perform complex analyses and generate publication-ready results without the need for software installation or coding expertise. Simply choose your analysis module, upload your dataset, and receive instant, insightful outputs.

RAISINS is developed by a group of young statisticians at Statoberry LLP, inspired and mentored by the Department of Agricultural Statistics, College of Agriculture, Vellayani, under Kerala Agricultural University. Statoberry LLP is a research-focused startup committed to making statistical computing more accessible, efficient, and impactful.

Each module in RAISINS is meticulously tested over months and reviewed by expert statisticians to ensure statistical accuracy and practical relevance.

Analysis Modules

Doing a statistical analysis has never been easier. Simply select the appropriate analysis module listed below to launch the application. Our platform operates on secure cloud infrastructure to ensure high-performance computing for your data analysis. To support sustained access and development, a modest subscription is required per module. These plans are thoughtfully structured to remain affordable for students and researchers. Once subscribed, access the app and upload your dataset and click Run Analysis the results are generated instantly, with accuracy and clarity.

Preview Mode

Explore any app for free using Preview Mode ‚Äî no registration needed. Just enter your email and OTP to begin. Use this mode for Explore, teaching, training programmes or hands-on practice. To upload your own data for analysis, a subscription is required. Model datasets are available within Preview Mode.

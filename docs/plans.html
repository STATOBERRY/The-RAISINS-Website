🧩 Module License

At RAISINS, we believe in accessibility and flexibility for all learners. That’s why each app is available as an independent module, allowing you to subscribe only to the tools you need. Whether you’re analyzing a simple test or running complex analysis modules, you can unlock any app on a monthly or 6-month basis, starting at just ₹199. This modular approach is perfect for individual students and researchers who need targeted analysis without the commitment of a full institutional plan. Simply click on the app listed under Analysis Modules and choose a monthly or 6-month license, starting at just ₹199. This modular subscription is perfect for students and researchers who need access to specific tools for a limited period.

💡 Module Licenses are specific to individual apps and are ideal for personal or single-user access. If you are affiliated with an academic or research institution and require full access to all RAISINS modules, please refer to the Institutional License section below and encourage your institution to partner with us.
